Arise is built on the principle that your data belongs to you — completely and unconditionally. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time, instantly, from within the app. There is no waiting period, no email required, and no hoops to jump through.

📱 ONLY METHOD

📱 Delete from Within the App

Delete all your cloud data, Google Drive backup, Firebase account, and local data in one step. Deletion is instant and permanent.

  1. Open the Arise app on your Android device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (⚙️) in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap "Delete Account".
  5. Read the confirmation dialog — it will show you exactly what data will be deleted.
  6. Verify your identity by re-authenticating with your Google account.
  7. Tap "Confirm Delete". Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

What Data Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account from within the app, the following data is permanently and irreversibly removed — all immediately:

Data Type Location Deleted? When
Habits, tasks, journal entries, notes, projects Google Drive (App Folder) YES Immediate
Username, email, profile picture (DP) Firebase Firestore YES Immediate
Level, rank, XP, health, gold, challenges Firebase Firestore YES Immediate
Firebase Authentication account Firebase Auth YES Immediate
Local app data (on-device DataStore) Your phone YES Immediate
Friends list and challenge history Firebase Firestore YES Immediate
Anonymized analytics data Firebase Analytics Anonymized (not linked to identity) Per Google's 14-month retention policy
Crash/performance logs Firebase Crashlytics Anonymized (not linked to identity) Per Google's 90-day retention policy

⚠️ Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming deletion.

Local Data Note

If you used Arise in Guest (Local) Mode without an account, your data is stored only on your device. To delete it, simply uninstall the app from your Android device. This will remove all locally stored app data.
