This Privacy Policy describes how Roshan Sharma ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the LifeLink mobile application ("App", "Service") available on the Google Play Store. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password. Passwords are handled exclusively by Firebase Authentication — we never see, store, or have access to your raw password.
  • Profile Information: Display name and profile picture, only if you choose to add or update them.
  • User-Generated Content: Tasks, habits, journal entries, notes, projects, areas, resources, and focus session settings. This content is stored locally on your device by default and synced to the cloud only if you opt in.
  • AI Chat Conversations: Messages you send to our AI assistant "Aria" are stored locally on your device. If you enable Cloud AI mode, your messages are sent to a third-party AI service for processing (see Section 5).
  • Survey Responses: If you complete the optional in-app onboarding survey, your responses (e.g., age, life expectancy estimate, personal goals) are stored locally on your device and used solely to personalize your experience.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymized, aggregated usage statistics such as screen views, feature usage frequency, session duration, and in-app events. This data is not linked to your personal identity and is used solely to understand how features are used and to improve the App.
  • Crash and Performance Data: We collect anonymized crash reports and performance metrics through Firebase to diagnose and fix technical issues. This includes device type, operating system version, app version, and stack traces. This data is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display non-personalized banner advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers and limited device information as described in Google's privacy policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's Google Settings. Ads are displayed based on in-app gamification health — users in good standing see fewer or no ads.

1.3 Information Accessed On-Device Only (Never Collected)

The following data is accessed and processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted, uploaded, or accessible to us or any third party:

  • App Usage Statistics: If you grant the Usage Access permission, LifeLink reads your screen time data to display your own usage patterns within the App. This data stays exclusively on your device.
  • Foreground App Detection: If you enable the Accessibility Service, LifeLink detects only which app is in the foreground to enforce your chosen screen time limits and app blocking rules. We explicitly set canRetrieveWindowContent=false — we never read screen content, text, passwords, or messages of any app.

Accessibility Service — Detailed Disclosure

LifeLink uses an Accessibility Service solely for the "Protocol" (Digital Discipline) feature. This service:

  • Does: Detect when a blocked or limited app comes to the foreground so that LifeLink can show a redirect/blocking overlay that you configured.
  • Does NOT: Read, collect, store, or transmit any screen content, text, passwords, form data, messages, or keystrokes. Our accessibility configuration explicitly sets canRetrieveWindowContent=false.
  • Does NOT: Operate when you have not granted the permission or when the Protocol feature is inactive.

This Accessibility Service is not used for analytics, advertising, data collection, or any purpose other than enforcing your voluntarily configured app-blocking and screen time rules.

5. AI Features and Data Processing

LifeLink includes an AI assistant called "Aria". You have complete control over how Aria processes your data:

5.1 Local AI Mode (Default)

All AI processing occurs on your device using Google MediaPipe. No data is sent to any external server. This is the default mode.

5.2 Cloud AI Mode (Opt-In)

If you explicitly enable Cloud AI mode, the following data may be sent to the Groq Cloud API (which runs the Llama 3.3 70B model) for processing:

  • Your chat messages
  • Conversation history for context
  • Player profile stats (level, rank, tasks completed, etc.) — only if you allow via AI Privacy Controls
  • Recent journal entries — only if you allow via AI Privacy Controls
  • Notes — only if you allow via AI Privacy Controls
  • Focus session/Protocol settings — only if you allow via AI Privacy Controls

This data is processed transiently by the API and is not persistently stored by the third-party AI provider beyond the duration of the API call. We encourage you to review Groq's Privacy Policy.

5.3 AI Privacy Controls

You can individually enable or disable Aria's access to each category of your data (player stats, journal, notes, protocol settings) via Settings → AI Privacy Control. You can switch between Local and Cloud AI at any time.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Android DataStore (preferences) and Room Database (SQLite). This storage is sandboxed by the Android operating system and is inaccessible to other apps.

6.2 Cloud Storage (Opt-In)

If you sign in and enable sync, your data is stored in:

  • Google Firebase Firestore: User account information, username, and player stats are stored in your private Firestore document, protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only authenticated users can access their own data.
  • Google Drive App Data Folder: Full app backups (tasks, habits, journal entries, projects, settings, and optional wallpaper) are stored in your Google Drive's hidden application data folder. Only LifeLink can access this folder — not even you can browse it via the Drive web interface, and we cannot access it either. This data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Google.

6.3 Security Measures

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text. Firebase Authentication handles all credential management using industry-standard encryption and hashing.
  • All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Firebase Security Rules enforce per-user data isolation.
  • Firebase App Check with Play Integrity is enabled to protect against abuse and unauthorized access.
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete it within the App or uninstall the App.
  • Cloud Data (Firebase): Retained while your account is active. Upon in-app account deletion, cloud data is permanently deleted after account verification.
  • Google Drive Backups: Retained in your personal Google Drive while your account is active. In-app account deletion deletes the app backup from Google Drive before deleting your account. If you request deletion outside the app, you may also remove the backup or revoke LifeLink's access from your Google Account settings.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized analytics data is retained per Google's data retention policies (default: 14 months, not linked to your identity).
  • AI Conversations: Stored locally on your device. Cloud AI API calls are transient — data is not retained by Groq beyond the API request.
